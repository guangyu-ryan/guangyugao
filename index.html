Guangyu(Ryan) Gao (高广宇)
Associate Professor,in Linc (Lab of INtelligent Computing),
School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Institute of Technology
from Oct. 2013, working with Chi Harold Liu.
  • Deputy Director, Institute of Data Science and Knowledge Engineering, BIT（2018-now）
  • Guest Asst. Director, Bureau of Economy and Inform.,  Shunyi District, Beijing. (2019.1-2020.2)
  • Asst. Director, National Lab. of Data Intelligence for China Light Industry (2014 - now)
  • Asst. Director, IBM Mainframe Excellence Center (Beijing) (2014 - now)
  • Asst. Director, IBM Big Data Technology Center (2014 - now)
I got my Ph.D. degree on Computer Science and Technology from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2013, supervised by Huadong Ma.
I also spent about one year at National University of Singapore as a government-sponsored Joint-Ph.D. student from 2012 to 2013, supervised by Shuicheng Yan.

Email: guangyu.ryan AT gmail.com / guangyugao AT bit.edu.cn
     

Research Interests: 
Computer Vision with Deep Learning (i.e., Action Recognition, Few Shot Segmentation, Person Search, Small Object Detection), 
Multimedia Computing (i.e. Cross-Modality Embedding, Multimedia Analysis) etc.
