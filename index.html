I got my Ph.D. degree on Computer Science and Technology from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2013, supervised by Huadong Ma.
I also spent about one year at National University of Singapore as a government-sponsored Joint-Ph.D. student from 2012 to 2013, supervised by Shuicheng Yan.
